# Off-Page SEO Strategy for LandlordHub

This guide outlines actionable strategies to improve your off-page SEO, which focuses on building authority and trust signals from external sources.

## üéØ What is Off-Page SEO?

Off-page SEO refers to activities done outside your website that impact your search rankings:
- **Backlinks** - Links from other websites pointing to yours
- **Social signals** - Social media presence and engagement
- **Brand mentions** - Getting mentioned without links
- **Local SEO** - Business listings and directories (if applicable)
- **Content marketing** - Creating shareable content

### 1. Build High-Quality Backlinks

#### **A. Industry Directories**
Submit LandlordHub to relevant directories:
- **PropTech Directories:**
  - PropTech Companies Directory
  - SaaS Directory
  - Real Estate Tech Directory
  
- **Software Directories:**
  - Product Hunt (submit when ready)
  - G2 Crowd (get reviews)
  - Capterra (get reviews)
  - Software Advice
  - GetApp
  - AlternativeTo

- **Business Directories:**
  - Crunchbase
  - AngelList
  - Clutch.co

#### **B. Guest Posting & Content Partnerships**
- Write guest posts for real estate blogs
- Partner with property management influencers
- Create valuable content that others want to link to

#### **C. Resource Pages**
- Create a "Resources" or "Blog" section
- Write guides like "Complete Guide to Property Management"
- Create downloadable resources (checklists, templates)
- These become linkable assets

#### **D. HARO (Help a Reporter Out)**
- Sign up for HARO queries
- Respond to journalist requests about property management
- Get mentioned in articles with links back
